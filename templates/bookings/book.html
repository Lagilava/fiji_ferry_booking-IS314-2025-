Need help with Schedule & Email Verification?

How to complete Step 1:

  1. Select your ferry route and departure time from the dropdown.
  2. Guests must verify email:
    1. Enter your email in the Email Address field.
    2. Click Send Code to receive a 6-digit code.
    3. Enter the code and click Verify. You’ll see “Email verified ✔” when successful.
  3. Click Next: Passengers to continue.

Important:

  • You cannot proceed to Step 2 unless your guest email is verified.
  • Verification codes typically arrive within a minute and may expire after a short time.
  • Changing the email address will clear verification; send/verify a new code.

Didn’t get the code?

  • Check your spam/junk folder.
  • Make sure your email is typed correctly; then tap Send Code again.
  • Wait 60 seconds before requesting another code.

About schedules:

  • Only upcoming schedules with available seats are shown.
  • The list updates automatically (watch for a brief “shimmer” when it refreshes).
  • If it’s close to departure, options may disappear as ferries leave or sell out.

Need help with Passengers?

How to complete Step 2:

  1. Enter the number of adults, children, and infants traveling.
  2. For each passenger, fill in their full name exactly as shown on their ID.
  3. Upload a clear photo or PDF of their passport or driver's license.
  4. Children and infants must be linked to an adult passenger.
  5. Click "Next: Add-Ons" when all passenger details are complete.
  • Names: Must match the ID document exactly.
  • ID Document: Upload a clear photo or PDF of passport/driver's license.
  • Children & Infants: Must be linked to an adult passenger in the dropdown.
  • Phone: Optional.

Adding a vehicle

  • Tick Add Vehicle to Booking if you are transporting a vehicle.
  • Select a vehicle type — pricing is based on the type you choose.
  • A license plate is required so our crew can identify your vehicle on board.

Adding cargo

  • Tick Add Cargo to Booking if you’re shipping items.
  • Select a cargo type and enter a weight > 0 kg.
  • Use the license plate field if your cargo is vehicle-mounted or needs an external ID.

Before you pay — modification policy

Changing a confirmed booking (date, passengers, or extras) incurs an additional modification fee, and any fare difference is charged or refunded. Bookings cannot be modified within 24 hours of the scheduled departure.

Need help with Review?

How to complete Step 4:

  1. Review all passenger details, selected schedule, and total cost.
  2. Choose your payment method (Card, ANZ Fiji, BSP, M-PAiSA, or MyCash).
  3. Check the privacy consent box to agree to terms.
  4. Click "Confirm & Pay Securely" to complete payment on a secure page.
  • Double-check: Ensure passenger names, IDs, and total price are correct.
  • Payment: Card payments use Stripe; bank and mobile-money options open their own secure gateway.
  • After payment: You'll receive a confirmation email with your ticket.
